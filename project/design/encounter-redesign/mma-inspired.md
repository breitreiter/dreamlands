key state properties:
* Initiative/momentum - the degree to which the pc is snowballing, chaining action, or pushing aggressively
* Control/dominance - the degree to which the pc currently controls the outcome of the conflict, with zero being "you have no control, you are just reacting"
* Spirits/energy - the reserve of mental/physical fortitude retained by the pc
* Resistance - the reserve of capability, challenge, or fortitude retained by the antagonist

verbs:
* Convert - risky attempt to create an opening (a fleeting opportunity) via Initiative burn
* Destabilize - risky attempt to create an opening (a fleeting opportunity) via Spirits burn
* Exploit - take advantage of an opening to gain Control OR damage Resistance
* Press - Convert Spirits into Initiative

initial approach:
* Scout - zero momentum, low control, fish for a free opening
* Direct - balanced
* Wild - high momentum, low control

closings:
* Resistance kill - you've eroded the last of the encounter's resistance
* Control kill - you've dominated so hard that victory is inevitable
* Spirits loss - you've run out of energy and suffer consequences
