## **Combat**

* **Unskilled** — Violence is a tool for simpletons and savages
* **Trained** — You know drills and forms, but have spilled little blood
* **Expert** — You read intent in a shoulder twitch and end fights decisively

---

## **Negotiation**

* **Unskilled** — You speak plainly and without artifice
* **Trained** — You recognize leverage and know when to press or yield
* **Expert** — You shape the discussion like an artisan

---

## **Bushcraft**

* **Unskilled** — The outdoors is strange, hostile terrain
* **Trained** — You can find water, shelter, and a way through
* **Expert** — You read land and weather like scripture

---

## **Cunning**

* **Unskilled** — You prefer a straightforward approach
* **Trained** — You notice angles and opportunities others overlook
* **Expert** — You are always three moves ahead 

---

## **Luck**

* **Unskilled** — You’ve learned to rely on skill, never chance
* **Trained** — Fortune sometimes favors you
* **Expert** — You live a charmed life

---

## **Mercantile**

* Unskilled — You have enthusiasm, but little sense of margins or risk
* Trained — You understand trade and the cost of tying up coin
* Expert — You walk markets like a lion, hungry and calculating
