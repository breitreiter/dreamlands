# Trade good taxonomy

---

# PLAINS

## Tier 1 — Heartland

**Blank Ledger Book** — A stiff-backed ledger with crisp lined pages and a faint watermark of Aldgate's scales pressed into every sheet. (small)

**Bolt of Undyed Wool Cloth** — A tightly rolled length of practical wool, coarse but even, bound with twine and sealed with a mill stamp. (small)

**Stamped Grain Weights** — A nested stack of palm-sized brass weights, each etched with imperial measures and worn smooth at the edges. (small)

**Silver trade bar** — A heavy silver bar. The intricate Merchant Guild stamp proves the purity of the metal. (medium)

---

## Tier 2 — Wildlands

**Army Surplus Spearheads** — Five oil-wrapped iron spearheads tied together with cord, their sockets still marked with faded regimental numbers. (small)

**Salvaged Signal Lantern** — A dented brass field lantern with intact shutter mechanism and soot still blackening the chimney glass. (small)

**Cracked Surveyor's Transit** — A tripod-mounted sighting instrument in a leather case, one lens fractured but the leveling screws still precise. (medium)

**Vitrified Stone Shards** — A cloth-wrapped parcel of unnaturally smooth, glass-sheened stone fragments fused by impossible heat. (small)

**Depot Iron Mess Kit** — A hinged iron cook-tin stamped with imperial inventory codes, scrubbed clean but permanently smoke-darkened. (small)

**Collapsed Tower Bell Fragment** — A curved section of bronze bell rim heavy in the hand, its casting inscription cut off mid-prayer. (small)

**Imperial Road Marker Plaque** — A cast-iron directional plaque pried from its post, bearing distances to forts that no longer exist. (small)

---

## Tier 3 — The Abandoned City

**Grid Regulator Plate** — A fist-sized brass and ceramic control plate engraved with a serial number and threaded ports for unseen machinery. (medium)

**Untouched Market Coin Chest** — A compact iron-bound coffer containing tightly stacked rolls of minted coin, each wrapped in paper bands. (large)

**Golem Memory Cylinder** — A palm-length crystal-and-brass cylinder humming faintly when held, its interior script rotating in slow sequence. (large)

---

# MOUNTAIN

## Tier 1 — Mining Folk

**Ridge Salt Slab** — A hand-cut brick of crystalline mountain salt wrapped in rough cloth and tied with hemp cord. (small)

**Charcoal Bundle** — A compact sack of dense hardwood charcoal, light but brittle, leaving fine black dust on the fingers. (small)

**Company Scrip Stack** — A leather pouch containing stamped tin tokens and folded promissory slips redeemable only at company stores. (small)

**Raw Silver Ore Chunk** — A jagged stone fist-sized and heavy, streaked through with dull veins that glint faintly in good light. (small)

---

## Tier 2 — Scholars

**Annotated Legal Codex** — A thick-bound volume crowded with marginalia in at least three disciplined hands. (medium)

**Precision Astrolabe** — A velvet-wrapped brass instrument of nested rotating rings etched with star tables and calibration marks. (medium)

**Printed Reform Pamphlets** — A neatly stitched stack of polemical essays printed on fine rag paper and trimmed square. (small)

**Sealed Research Dossier** — A wax-sealed folio of diagrams and correspondence stamped with institutional insignia. (medium)

**Engineered Retort Glassware** — A padded roll containing precisely blown glass bulbs and tubes fitted with corked joints. (medium)

**Corrected Mine Safety Blueprint** — A carefully inked technical drawing rolled into a leather tube and annotated with cautious revisions. (small)

**Observatory Lens Disc** — A thick polished crystal disc wrapped in felt, flawless at its center and cold as mountain water. (large)

---

## Tier 3 — The Stift

**Filed Petition (Year 73)** — A ribbon-bound stack of parchment forms bearing stamps, counterstamps, and several adjournment notices. (small)

**Binding Judgment Writ** — A heavy vellum decree embossed with a raised seal whose authority is felt before it is read. (medium)

**Wegtafel Fragment** — A broken stone directional tablet etched with carved arrows that no longer point anywhere useful. (small)

---

# FOREST

## Tier 1 — Forest Folk

**Stacked Firewood Bundle** — A neatly tied armful of seasoned oak lengths cut to uniform size. (small)

**Tanned Deerhide** — A supple, smoke-cured hide folded tight, its grain even and free of rot. (small)

**Charcoal Sack** — A small waxed sack of blackened wood chunks sized for steady heat. (small)

**Pitch Resin Pot** — A stoppered clay jar of thick pine pitch that softens and sharpens in the warmth of the hand. (small)

---

## Tier 2 — Deep Wood Exiles

**Ghostcap Extract Vials** — Corked glass vials of cloudy tincture that smells faintly sweet. (medium)

**Figured Heartwood Planks** — Carefully cut boards no longer than a forearm, their grain rippling in rare patterns. (medium)

**Luck Charms** — Palm-sized tokens of carved bone bound in copper wire and hung on a knotted cord. (small)

**Dryad's Knot Fungus** — A dried spiral of woody fungus wrapped in cloth, prized for its alchemical properties. (medium)

**Trail Kit Roll** — A compact leather roll containing blaze knives, resin markers, and coils of treated twine. (small)

**Unusual Local Writings** — A rough-bound journal of unusual observations from one of the more eccentric woodland exiles. (small)

**Caged Songbird** — A small, iridescent blue noctournal bird, praised for its haunting song. (medium)

---

## Tier 3 — The Hunter

**Unsettling Wooden Doll** — A crude, hand-made doll founding hanging from a cord in the deep woods. (small)

**Imperial Standard** — An antique banner in remarkable condition, sparkling with gold thread. (large)

**Master Snare Mechanism** — A compact spring-loaded trapping device engineered for silent, repeatable deployment. (small)

---

# SCRUB

## Tier 1 — Plateau Clans

**Caravan Spice Pouch** — A hefty drawstring pouch of aromatic dried petals and seeds blended in clan-specific proportions. (medium)

**Hammered Copper Armbands** — A box of flexible copper bands worked by hand and stamped with a subtle clan sigil. (small)

**Perfumed Resin Cakes** — Thumb-thick discs of hardened aromatic resin that release scent when warmed. (small)

**Exquisite Kaftan** — A fine garmet, lightweight yet elegant. (medium)

---

## Tier 2 — Kesharat Administration

**Rail Line Survey** — A hefty book documenting the extensive rail lines across the Kesharat empire. (small)

**Jade Slab** — An unworked slab of jade, wrapped in heavy cloth. (large)

**Signal Tower Clockwork Core** — A dense brass gear assembly housed in a padded wooden case. (medium)

**The Lattice, An Introduction** — A heavy volume of collected essays  explaining the Lattice in patient, circular prose. (medium)

**Stamped Rail Spikes** — Four heavy iron spikes bound together, each head marked with foundry stamps. (small)

**Kesharat Robe** — A heavy woolen robe with unusual geometric patterns. (medium)

**Confiscated Clan Banner** — A carefully folded length of dyed fabric bearing a clan emblem stitched in bold thread. (small)

**Unusual Coins** — A box of strange coins from distant lands. Not accepted as currency here, but possibly of interest to collectors. (small)

---

## Tier 3 — The Lattice

**Alignment Rod** — A smooth metal rod that hums faintly and vibrates in the hand when held upright. (medium)

**Colorless Crystal Node** — A multifaceted crystal that seems to refract light without producing any color. (medium)

**Worker Identification Band** — A metal wrist band engraved with a serial designation and fitted with an unremovable clasp. (small)

---

# SWAMP

## Tier 1 — Wetland Towns

**Bundled River Reeds** — A tight coil of dried river reeds trimmed evenly and bound for weaving or roofing. (small)

**Peat Fuel Brick** — A dense rectangular brick of dried peat wrapped in oilcloth to keep it intact. (small)

**Leech Jar** — A corked glass jar containing several dark, sluggish leeches in cloudy water. (small)

**Resin-Sealed Waterproof Satchel** — A treated leather pouch whose seams are sealed in black pitch against water. (small)

---

## Tier 2 — Specialists & River Traders

**Embalming Salts** — A heavy leather drawstring bag of fine mineral salts blended to slow decay. (small)

**Blackwater Dye Vial** — A stoppered bottle of deep, inky dye that stains cloth almost permanently. (medium)

**Venom Ampoules** — A padded case holding two sealed glass ampoules filled with pale yellow venom. (small)

**Bog-Iron Ingot** — A compact, rough-cast iron bar smelted from marsh deposits, pitted but solid. (small)

**Preserved Lotus Resin** — A wax-wrapped box of hardened resin nodes used in ritual and scenting. (medium)

**Witchfire Lamp** — An intricate glass terrarium containing a moss that gives off a pale blue light. (large)

**Nightbloom Poison Resin** — A wax-sealed clar jar containing a tar-dark resin that stains the skin. (small)

---

## Tier 3 - The Swamp Itself

**Marsh Pearl** — A fist-sized irregular pearl with shifting iridescence and a faint organic warmth. (large)

**Root Head** — A life-sized replica of your own head, formed from a twisted knot of tiny roots. (medium)
