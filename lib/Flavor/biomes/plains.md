# Tier 2 ‚Äî Overreach & Residue

### Philosophy:

Order becomes extractive.

This is the failed campaign zone.

The empire built:

* Forts.
* Signal towers.
* Supply depots.
* Roadworks.

Then retreated.

Now what remains is:

* Skeletonized infrastructure.
* Half-collapsed garrisons.
* Dug-in artillery pits.
* Watchfires never relit.

---

### Social Ecology

There are no towns.

Only:

* Outposts barely functioning.
* Rag-tag patrols.
* Affable scavenger bands.
* Ex-soldiers who never rejoined civilian life.

The tone is brittle.

Everyone is defensive.

Everyone believes the land still belongs to someone.

# Tier 3 ‚Äî Autonomous Order

### Philosophy:

No settlements.

No civilians.

No bandits.

No patrols.

This is not a battlefield.

---

### The Abandoned City

This is like a mirror of Aldgate. This was someone else's city on their frontier. the machines were there to protect the civilians. the civilians are gone, but the machines are still there. they defend nothing.

Empty.

Not destroyed.

Not looted.

Just vacated.

Doors closed.

Granaries empty.
Market stalls intact.

The empire ran the numbers and left it behind. it wasn't worth the cost to go out and deactivate the defense grid.

---

### The Grid

Not chaotic.

Not malicious.

It activates based on:

* Density thresholds.
* Weapon signatures.
* Heat signatures.
* Boundary crossing.

It does not chase beyond parameters.

It enforces.
