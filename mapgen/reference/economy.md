# classes of goods

Nearly all goods:
- can be bought from suppliers or found in encounters
- cheap and plentiful near the starter area, expensive and rare in the far lands

## Food 
Everything edible, from rutabagas to salt pork to grain
- can be eaten

## Tools
Picks, saws, hammers, rope, nails, common equipment
- can resolve some encounters

## Medicine
Herbs, bandages, poultices, tonics
- can be used to heal injuries

## Martial Goods
Swords, spears, bows, arrows, shields, armor
- can be equipped to become more fighty

## Textiles
Cloth, wool, leather, rope, finished garments
- no in-game use

## Luxuries 
Jewelry, artwork, fine furniture, perfume, spices
- no in-game use

## Books
Scrolls, tomes, maps, documents, rare manuscripts
- some do nothing
- some reveal distant POIs
- some unlock special options in encounters
