# Hills encounters by tier
# Tiers: 0-15 (Safe), 16-25 (Frontier), 26-40 (Wildlands), 40+ (Dark Places)

# Tier 1: Safe zone - peaceful
0-15:A shepherd tends their flock on the hillside.
0-15:Goats scatter at your approach.
0-18:A patrol surveys the land from above.
0-20:Miners haul ore from a nearby dig.

# Tier 2: Frontier - mixed
10-30:Wild boars root in the underbrush.
12-30:A hermit watches from a cave entrance.
15-35:You spot an old watchtower in the distance.

# Tier 3: Wildlands - dangerous
20-45:Mountain lions prowl the rocky slopes.
25-50:Raiders use the high ground for an ambush.
25-45:A rockslide blocks the path ahead.

# Tier 4: Dark Places - hostile
35-999:Stone trolls lurk among the boulders.
40-999:The hills themselves seem to shift and move.
40-999:Ancient burial mounds glow with pale light.
