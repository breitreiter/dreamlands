# Forest encounters by tier
# Tiers: 0-15 (Safe), 16-25 (Frontier), 26-40 (Wildlands), 40+ (Dark Places)

# Tier 1: Safe zone - peaceful
0-15:A woodcutter works alone in a clearing.
0-15:Elusive deer vanish into the undergrowth.
0-18:Charcoal burners tend their kilns.
0-20:A forester marks trees for harvest.

# Tier 2: Frontier - mixed
10-30:A pack of wolves shadows your movements.
12-30:An enormous bear blocks the path.
15-35:A wounded traveler calls for help.

# Tier 3: Wildlands - dangerous
20-45:Bandits have made camp nearby.
25-50:Strange lights flicker between the trees.
25-45:You find signs of a large predator's territory.

# Tier 4: Dark Places - hostile
35-999:Giant spiders have webbed the path ahead.
40-999:The trees whisper in no language you know.
40-999:Eyes watch from every shadow.
