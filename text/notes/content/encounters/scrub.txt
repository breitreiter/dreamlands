# Scrub encounters by tier
# Tiers: 0-15 (Safe), 16-25 (Frontier), 26-40 (Wildlands), 40+ (Dark Places)

# Tier 1: Safe zone - environmental challenges
0-15:Cacti provide meager shelter from the sun.
0-18:A dried-up well marks an abandoned camp.
0-20:Desperate refugees flee across the wasteland.

# Tier 2: Frontier - mixed
10-30:Vultures circle something up ahead.
12-30:A dust storm approaches rapidly.
15-35:You find tracks of a large predator.

# Tier 3: Wildlands - dangerous
20-45:Giant lizards bask on the hot rocks.
25-50:Raiders on horseback spot you from afar.
25-45:The heat becomes unbearable.

# Tier 4: Dark Places - deadly
35-999:A giant scorpion emerges from the sand.
40-999:Mirages reveal impossible, beckoning shapes.
40-999:The land itself seems to drain your strength.
