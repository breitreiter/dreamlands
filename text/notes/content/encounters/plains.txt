# Plains encounters by tier
# Tiers: 0-15 (Safe), 16-25 (Frontier), 26-40 (Wildlands), 40+ (Dark Places)

# Tier 1: Safe zone - peaceful
0-15:Farmers tend their fields nearby.
0-15:A traveling merchant's cart approaches on the road.
0-18:A patrol of soldiers marches past.
0-20:Pilgrims journey to a distant shrine.

# Tier 2: Frontier - mixed
10-30:A herd of wild horses gallops across the grassland.
12-30:A dust cloud marks riders approaching fast.
15-35:A lone hunter tracks game through the grass.

# Tier 3: Wildlands - dangerous
20-45:Bandits lurk in the tall grass ahead.
25-50:A lone wolf watches from a distance.
25-45:Wild dogs scavenge near an abandoned camp.

# Tier 4: Dark Places - hostile
35-999:A pack of dire wolves circles your position.
40-999:Something massive moves beneath the earth.
40-999:The grass itself seems to reach for you.
