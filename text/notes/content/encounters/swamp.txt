# Swamp encounters by tier
# Tiers: 0-15 (Safe), 16-25 (Frontier), 26-40 (Wildlands), 40+ (Dark Places)

# Tier 1: Safe zone - minor hazards
0-15:Poisonous snakes slither through the reeds.
0-18:A hermit offers guidance for a price.
0-20:You find a boardwalk through the mire.

# Tier 2: Frontier - mixed
10-30:A crocodile lurks just beneath the surface.
12-30:Giant leeches swarm from the murky water.
15-35:Strange sounds echo across the marsh.

# Tier 3: Wildlands - dangerous
20-45:Will-o'-wisps lead travelers astray.
25-50:Lizardfolk hunters track your movement.
25-45:The ground gives way to quicksand.

# Tier 4: Dark Places - deadly
35-999:A coven of witches performs dark rituals.
40-999:Shambling undead rise from the muck.
40-999:The swamp water turns black and begins to bubble.
