# Mountain encounters by tier
# Tiers: 0-15 (Safe), 16-25 (Frontier), 26-40 (Wildlands), 40+ (Dark Places)

# Tier 1: Safe zone - challenging but safe
0-15:Mountain goats leap across impossible gaps.
0-18:A lost climber needs rescue.
0-20:You find a well-marked mountain trail.

# Tier 2: Frontier - mixed
10-30:An avalanche rumbles in the distance.
12-30:A giant eagle circles overhead.
15-35:You discover an abandoned mining camp.

# Tier 3: Wildlands - dangerous
20-45:Hostile monks defend their monastery.
25-50:Ice trolls guard a narrow pass.
25-45:A blizzard descends without warning.

# Tier 4: Dark Places - deadly
35-999:Stone giants hurl boulders from above.
40-999:A dragon's roar echoes through the peaks.
40-999:The mountain itself seems to reject your presence.
